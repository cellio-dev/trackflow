Integrations

Plex

Often 4 — check your Plex library URL.

When enabled, the login page shows Sign in with Plex. New Plex sign-ins are added as users with provider Plex and role User.

When enabled, each Plex Sync job asks Plex Media Server to refresh the music library, runs a TrackFlow filesystem library scan, maps Plex rating keys for playlist sync, then syncs followed playlists.

When enabled, users who sign in with Plex use recent music play history from your configured Plex server to seed Discover track and artist recommendations instead of followed artists. Plays count only when at least half of the track was heard (short skips are ignored).
